📜 Leon County Roleplay Rules

All members are expected to follow these rules to maintain a professional and realistic RP environment.

⭐ General Rules

• Respect all players, staff, and departments.
• No harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior.
• Follow staff instructions at all times.
• Use common sense and maintain professionalism.

🎭 Roleplay Rules

• Stay in character during roleplay situations.
• No RDM (Random Deathmatch).
• No VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch).
• No FailRP or unrealistic actions.
• Value your character's life.

🚔 Law Enforcement Rules

• Follow department procedures and training.
• Use proper radio communication.
• Do not abuse equipment or authority.
• Maintain professionalism while on duty.

🚒 Fire & EMS Rules

• Provide realistic emergency services.
• Work together with other departments.
• Maintain proper scene safety.

🏙️ Civilian Rules

• Create realistic characters.
• Follow server laws and roleplay standards.
• Do not intentionally ruin other players' experiences.

⚠️ Staff Actions

Staff may take action against players who violate server rules. Punishments may include warnings, removals, or bans depending on the situation.
